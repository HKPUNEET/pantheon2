OPERATIONAL BRIEFING

WHAT IS A CASE COMPETITION?

A case competition is a high-level academic and professional simulation where teams of students are presented with a real-life business problem faced by an organization.

Within a limited time frame, teams must conduct market research, analyze operational data, construct financial sheets, and formulate executable strategies. The event culminates in a presentation where teams pitch their solutions directly to a panel of expert corporate judges.

STAGE 01
The Dossier Release

Teams receive a detailed briefing package detailing a real company's market challenges and financial reports.

STAGE 02
Strategic Formulation

Teams collaborate to identify core problems, run financial projections, and design high-impact solutions.

STAGE 03
The Executive Pitch

Candidates pitch their recommendations slide deck to a board of corporate directors and answer critical inquiries.
